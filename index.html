Our Story

Passion for beauty,
perfected over 19 years

"If you are looking for flawless skin and beauty services, Mahek Beauty Salon is where you need to be."

Welcome to Mahek Beauty Salon. For nearly two decades we have been proudly serving the Greater Toronto Area from our Etobicoke and Brampton location, offering the full range of latest beauty and grooming services.

Our passionate, licensed team takes time to understand your hair type, skin needs, and personal goals before recommending any treatment. From everyday haircuts to transformative bridal packages, we bring the same care and precision to every chair.

We serve both men and women, and keep our environment spotlessly clean, calm, and welcoming — the kind of place you keep coming back to.
